Controls

  • Camera: Left click + drag to rotate, right click + drag to pan, scroll to zoom
  • Asteroid Mode: Click ☄️ to enter placement mode
  • Mouse: Move to position asteroid hologram
  • SPACEBAR: Summon asteroid at hologram position
  • Arrow Keys: Set velocity direction
